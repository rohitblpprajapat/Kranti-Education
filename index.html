    “NECESSITY IS THE MOTHER OF INVENTION “

    -There was a need felt to develop the CALIBER of children for the bright future of the Nation. This led to the establishment of the CALIBER ACADEMY SENIOR SECONDARY SCHOOL a co-educational English medium school run by Caliber Shikhan Sansthan.
    A Child is just like clay which can be moulded in any shape, either good or bad and what gives shape in education provided by parents, teachers and environment. Infact the child education Is the seed of Nation, Which changes it into a fruitful tree.
    The school is nested in the lap of vast expanse of lush green land with calm and quite surrounding. A vast and accomodious ground of 15000 sqm. located in chittorgarh. It is well equipped with the required infrastructure containing well established computer Lab and well furnitured airy classrooms.
    We emphasize on smart classes, projector based education and abacus learning at the high level.
    In this new era, every parents wants their ward to be an all rounder. Their desire to see them as genuine and smart so that he/she may stand among many people without hesitation and can face the practical aspects of life in future For this the school provides a food number of opportunities in co-curricular activities for all round development of the students. Our total functioning aiming is to inculcate basis values of life and its purpose.
